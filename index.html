Moraine lake

Lake in North America

Moraine Lake is a glacially-fed lake in Banff National Park, 14 kilometers outside the Village of Lake Louise, Alberta, Canada. It is situated in the Valley of the Ten Peaks, the Lake louise at an elevation of approximately 6,183 feet.

Surface elevation: 1,884m

Area: 50 ha
